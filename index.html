Program Overview

This program is designed to help you sculpt and strengthen your abdominal muscles. With a combination of bodyweight exercises and strategic rest, you will see results quickly. Follow along with the daily routines to achieve your goals!

Top Exercises for Abs

1. Plank

Hold for 30 seconds, increase time as you get stronger. This exercise helps improve your core stability and endurance.

2. Crunches

Perform 3 sets of 20 reps to target your upper abs. Keep your movements controlled to maximize the burn and avoid neck strain.

3. Leg Raises

Perform 3 sets of 15 reps to target your lower abs. Focus on controlling your legs as you raise them to really activate the core.

4. Bicycle Crunches

Perform 3 sets of 30 seconds. This exercise targets both your upper and lower abs while engaging your obliques for a full-core workout.

5. Mountain Climbers

Perform 3 sets of 30 seconds. A great exercise for building endurance and burning fat, as well as strengthening the core.

6. Russian Twists

Perform 3 sets of 20 reps on each side. This exercise works your obliques and helps improve rotational movement and flexibility.

7. Reverse Crunches

Perform 3 sets of 15 reps. Focus on pulling your hips toward your chest rather than swinging your legs up to avoid using momentum.

8. V-Situps

Perform 3 sets of 15 reps. This dynamic movement engages both your upper and lower abs, as well as your hip flexors.

9. Flutter Kicks

Perform 3 sets of 30 seconds. Flutter kicks work the lower abs and hip flexors, promoting a leaner midsection.

10. Dead Bug

Perform 3 sets of 15 reps. This is a great exercise for beginners to improve coordination and engage the entire core.

11. Toe Touches

12. Side Plank

Hold for 30 seconds on each side. The side plank helps build your obliques and improve overall core strength.

13. Hanging Leg Raises

Perform 3 sets of 12-15 reps. This is a more advanced exercise that targets the lower abs and helps improve grip strength.

14. Ab Rollouts

Perform 3 sets of 12 reps. Using an ab roller or barbell, this exercise focuses on the entire core, including the deeper muscles.

15. Sit-ups

Perform 3 sets of 20 reps. A classic core exercise that strengthens the entire abdominal region, helping build functional core strength.
